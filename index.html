Voice Change for Assertive Sentence


1.Present Indefinite Tense:
S+V1/Vs/es+O.
=(O--S)+am/is/are+V3+by+(S--O).
a) He reads a book.
=A book is read by him.
2.Present Continuous Tense:
S+am/is/are+Ving+O.
=(O--S)+am/is/are+being+V3+by+(S--O).
3. Present Perfect Tense:
S+have/has+V3+O.
=(O--S)+have/has+been+V3+by+(S--O.)
Ex: He has read a book.
=A book has been read by him.
4.Past Indefinite Tense:
S+V2+O.
=(O--S)+was/were+V3+by+(S--O).
Ex: He ate rice.
=Rice was eaten by him.
5. Past Continuous Tense:
S+was/were+Ving+O.
=(O--S)+was/were+being+V3+by+(S--O.)
Ex: He was eating rice.
=Rice was being eaten by him.
6.Past Perfect Tense:
S+had+V3+O.
=(O--S) +had + been +V3+(S--O).
Ex: He had eaten rice.
=Rice had been eaten by him.
7. Future Indefinite Tense:
S+shall/will+V1+O.
=(O--S)+shall/will+be+V+3by+(S--O).
Ex: He will write a letter.
=A letter will be written by him.
8. Future Continuous Tense:
S+shall/will+be+Ving+O.
=(O--S)+shall/will+be+being+V3+(S--O).
Ex: He will be doing the work.
=The work will be being done by him.
09. Future Perfect Tense:
S+shall/will+have+V3+O.
=(O--S)+shall/will+have+been+V3 +by+(S--O).
Ex: He will have written a letter.
=A letter will have been written by him.

Voice Change for Imperative Sentence


1. V+O.
=Let+(O--S)+be+V3.
Ex: Do the work.
=Let the work be done.
2. Do/did/does+not+V+O.
=Let +not+O+be+V3.
Ex:Don't do the work.
=Let not the work be done.
3.Let+O1+V+O2.
=Let+O2+be+V3+by-by+O1.
Ex:Let him do the work.
=Let the work be done by him.
4.Never, V+O.
=Let never+ (O--S)+be+V3.
Ex: Never tell a lie.
= Let never a lie be told.
